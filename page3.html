New items

On July 18, 2018, the International Olympic Committee announced that seven events will be added to the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympic Games, and a total of 109 gold medals will be produced at that time. At the same time, the proportion of female athletes is further improved, and the proportion of male and female athletes tends to be more balanced

The IOC Executive Board meeting held in Lausanne, Switzerland, adopted a plan on the project setting of the Beijing Winter Olympic Games. The newly added seven sub events are women's single snowmobile, short track speed skating mixed group relay, ski jumping mixed group, Freestyle Ski big jump (men and women), freestyle skiing air skills mixed group and snowboarding obstacle chasing mixed group
