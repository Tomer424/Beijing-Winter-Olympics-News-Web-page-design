❆ Preparation process

Shift of center of gravity 2015-2017Cycle on 2018-2019Sprint 2020-2022

On July 31, 2015, Wang Anshun, then mayor of Beijing, made it clear at the press conference after the election that Beijing officially changed from the stage of bid for the Olympic Games to the stage of preparation. After the meeting, Wang Hui, director of the Publicity Department of the Winter Olympic bid committee, said that the Winter Olympic Organizing Committee would be officially established in five months

On December 15, 2015, the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics Organizing Committee was established. At 3 p.m. on the 15th, the inaugural meeting was held in the Great Hall of the people. Earlier, Wu Jingmi, Executive Deputy Secretary General of the Beijing Winter Olympic bid committee, said, "the Winter Olympic Organizing Committee is expected to have 28 professional departments, plus the operation centers of Yanqing and Zhangjiakou. The scale of the Winter Olympic Organizing Committee is very small in the early stage of its establishment, and the largest scale after the start of the competition is equivalent to one-third of the Organizing Committee of the 2008 Summer Olympic Games."

In 2016, the construction of the national speed skating museum began

On February 24, 2016, IOC President Bach announced the list of members of the Coordination Committee for the 2022 Winter Olympic Games in Beijing. Zhukov, a member of the International Olympic Committee and a Russian who once served as the chairman of the evaluation committee of the bidding city for the 2022 Winter Olympic Games, served as the chairman of the Coordination Committee. The Coordination Committee has a total of 16 members. In addition to Zhukov, there are president Casper of the International Ski Federation, President Keith nice of the World Curling Federation, President cherneshenko of the Organizing Committee of the 2014 Sochi Winter Olympic Games, President of the Hong Kong Olympic Committee of China and vice president of the Asian Olympic Council Huo Zhenting.

He stressed that it is of great significance and responsibility to make good preparations for the Beijing Winter Olympic Games and the winter Paralympic Games. We should enhance our sense of mission and responsibility, conscientiously implement the development concept of innovation, coordination, green, openness and sharing, adhere to green, shared, open and clean Olympics, complete all preparatory tasks with high standards and high quality, and turn the Beijing Winter Olympic Games and winter Paralympic Games into a wonderful and extraordinary Olympic event to the people of the motherland Submit a satisfactory answer to the international community.

On October 10, 2016, the Coordination Committee of the International Olympic Committee Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics held its first meeting in Beijing

He stressed that preparing for the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics is a major event for the country. All relevant local departments should focus on hosting a wonderful and extraordinary Olympic Games, formulate scientific and reasonable plans, make economical and intensive use of resources, and complete all tasks of the preparatory work with high quality according to the progress.
